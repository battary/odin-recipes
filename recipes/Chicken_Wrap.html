Chicken Wrap

A quick high protein meal made with chicken seasoned with a simple spice mix, lettuce, an easy to make high protein yogurt sauce, and wrapped in a high protein lavash bread

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Non-Fat Yogurt
  • Low-Fat Mayo
  • Slice of Lemon
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Cook chicken
  2. Mix mayo, yogurt, lemon juice, and spices to make sauce
  3. Spread yogurt sauce on lavash bread
  4. Add chicken, cheese, and lettuce to wrap
  5. Wrap in tin foil
  6. Cut in half
  7. Enjoy!
